What is a freight forwarder TMS?

A freight forwarder TMS (transportation management system) is software that runs the day to day operations of a freight forwarding business: building rates and quotes, booking shipments, filing documents, tracking containers, billing customers, and reporting on margin. Unlike a generic TMS designed for shippers or carriers, a forwarder TMS is built around the forwarder workflow, where you buy and resell transportation, coordinate multiple carriers, and manage both the operations and the back office in one place.

GoFreight is an all in one freight forwarder TMS. It replaces the patchwork of spreadsheets, carrier portals, and accounting tools that small and growing forwarders usually rely on, so your team works from a single source of truth.
