Executive Summary

Month in Review

March 2026 continued GoFreight's upward trajectory with 5,067 total clicks (+14.5% MoM), marking the third consecutive month of click growth. Impressions dipped to 1,747,346 (-9.2%), but this is a healthy correction as CTR improved from 0.23% to 0.29% and average position improved from 8.4 to 7.7. Key drivers:

  • Homepage recovery: Homepage clicks surged +25.1% (1,031 to 1,290), reversing February's decline
  • Branded bounce back: Branded clicks recovered +27.5% (1,016 to 1,295), reversing the February decline
  • Non-branded growth continues: Non-branded clicks grew +10.7% (3,408 to 3,772), sustained organic discovery
  • Glossary pages reactivated: Glossary clicks went from 0 (Feb, filtered) to 26 clicks with 8,161 impressions after migration to main domain
  • Commercial content surge: best-tms-software (+137% impressions) and best-freight-management-software (+216% impressions) are capturing commercial intent

AEO Performance: Strong Momentum Continues

March delivered across all AI visibility metrics, including a dramatic reversal in LLM engagement. Key observations:

  • Visibility climbed to 25.3% (+1.8pp): Fourth consecutive month of gains, narrowing the gap to top 3 competitors
  • LLM Engaged Sessions exploded +111%: From 54 to 114, completely reversing February's 22% decline. Users from AI sources are now engaging deeply with GoFreight content
  • Key Events surged to 18 (+800%): From 2 events in February to 18 in March, demonstrating that AI traffic is converting into meaningful business actions
  • Commercial content driving AI traffic: Best TMS Software (+162%) and Best Freight Management Software (+25%) are now top AI referrer pages, a shift from purely educational content
  • CargoWise pricing dipped (-26%): Natural fluctuation as AI traffic diversifies across more landing pages

Gap to close: GoFreight is now at 25.3% visibility vs CargoWise (~34%), Magaya (~32%), and Descartes (~30%). The gap is 5 to 9 points. With hub pages and FAQ sections planned for April, breaking into the top 3 by Q2 remains achievable.
