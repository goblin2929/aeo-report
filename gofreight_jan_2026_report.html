AEO Performance: Flat Month-over-Month

AI visibility metrics remained relatively flat in January compared to December. Key observations:

  • Visibility stable at ~14%: No significant gains or losses in AI response inclusion
  • Mentions held steady (29): Tied with Magaya but trailing in visibility % (14.3% vs 30.2%)
  • Position unchanged (~51): Mentions appearing mid-to-lower in AI responses

Why flat? AEO gains typically lag SEO content by 2-3 months as LLMs update their training data and citation sources. January SEO improvements (blog migration, new content) should reflect in AI visibility by late Q1.

AEO Focus Areas for February

  • Improve position in AI responses by strengthening authority signals (schema, citations)
  • Create more comparison content (GoFreight vs Magaya, GoFreight vs CargoWise)
  • Perplexity showing strongest performance (19.1%) - lean into citation-friendly content
  • Add LLM share links to footer for better AI discoverability
