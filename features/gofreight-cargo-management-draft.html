Frequently Asked Questions

What is cargo management software?
Cargo management software provides freight forwarders with tools to manage the complete shipment lifecycle‚Äîfrom initial booking through documentation, tracking, and final delivery. It handles both ocean and air cargo, including consolidation workflows.
Does GoFreight handle both ocean and air cargo?
Yes. GoFreight manages both ocean cargo (FCL, LCL, consolidation) and air cargo (HAWB/MAWB, flights) in one platform. You can track shipments across modes from a single dashboard.
Can I manage LCL consolidations?
Yes. GoFreight supports LCL consolidation workflows, allowing you to group multiple shippers into a single container. The system handles house bills, master bills, and split invoicing.
How does cargo tracking work?
GoFreight integrates with 125+ carriers for real-time tracking data. Container and air shipment status updates automatically, and you can set alerts for key milestones like vessel arrival, customs clearance, and delivery.
