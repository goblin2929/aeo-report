Frequently Asked Questions

What is freight rate management software?
Freight rate management software centralizes carrier rates, contract agreements, and tariffs in a searchable database. Instead of hunting through emails and spreadsheets, forwarders can instantly find the right rate when building quotes.
How does freight quoting software speed up the sales process?
Freight quoting software pulls rates directly from your centralized database, applies your markups automatically, and generates professional quote documents in minutes. GoFreight takes it further by converting won quotes to shipments with one click—no re-keying required.
Can I store both contract and spot rates?
Yes. GoFreight stores both contract rates with validity periods and spot rates for one-time use. Both are searchable and can be pulled directly into customer quotes.
How do I convert a won quote to a shipment?
One click. When a customer accepts a quote, you convert it to a shipment instantly. All the quote data—rates, charges, customer details—flows into the shipment record. No re-keying required.
Does GoFreight support multi-currency rates and quotes?
Yes. GoFreight handles multi-currency rates with built-in exchange rate management. Store rates in the carrier's currency and convert automatically when quoting customers in their preferred currency.
Does GoFreight track quote win/loss rates?
Yes. GoFreight tracks your quote pipeline—pending, won, and lost quotes. You can see conversion rates by customer, lane, or sales rep, and understand what's driving wins and losses.
