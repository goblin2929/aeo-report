Frequently Asked Questions

What is ISF filing?
ISF (Importer Security Filing), also known as 10+2, is a U.S. Customs requirement for ocean imports. Importers or their agents must submit detailed cargo information at least 24 hours before loading at the foreign port. ISF filing software automates this process.
Can I file AES directly from GoFreight?
Yes. GoFreight includes direct AES (Automated Export System) filing for ocean and air exports. Export shipment data flows directly into the filing, and you receive confirmation through the system.
How does data flow from shipments to filings?
When you create a shipment in GoFreight, the relevant data (shipper, consignee, commodity, HTS codes) automatically populates the filing form. You review, make any adjustments, and submit‚Äîno re-keying required.
Does GoFreight connect to CBP/ACE?
Yes. GoFreight connects directly to CBP's ACE (Automated Commercial Environment) system via ABI (Automated Broker Interface). Filings are submitted electronically and responses flow back into GoFreight.
