Frequently Asked Questions

What is a digital freight platform?
A digital freight platform provides freight forwarders with modern, cloud-based tools to manage operations electronically‚Äîfrom online quoting and booking through digital documentation and customer portals. It replaces paper-based workflows with efficient digital processes.
How does digitization improve freight forwarding?
Digitization reduces manual data entry, eliminates paper documents, speeds up quote turnaround, provides customers with self-service access, and enables remote work. Forwarders using digital platforms typically see significant efficiency improvements.
Can my customers access shipment information online?
Yes. GoFreight includes a branded customer portal where shippers can track shipments, download documents, view invoices, and receive automated notifications‚Äîall without calling your team.
Does GoFreight support paperless operations?
Yes. GoFreight enables fully digital operations with electronic document generation (BOL, AWB, commercial docs), digital signatures, online invoicing, and automated customer communications. Paper is optional, not required.
