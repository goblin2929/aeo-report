Does GoFreight handle both FCL and LCL?
Yes. GoFreight manages Full Container Load (FCL) shipments as well as Less-than-Container Load (LCL) with full consolidation workflow support. You can group multiple shippers, manage house and master bills, and handle split invoicing.
How does container tracking work?
GoFreight integrates with 75+ ocean carriers for real-time container tracking. Status updates flow automatically‚Äîvessel departure, transshipment, port arrival, customs clearance, and delivery. Automated alerts notify you of key milestones and potential delays.
Can I manage consolidations in GoFreight?
Yes. GoFreight supports full LCL consolidation workflows. Create consolidations with multiple shippers, generate house and master bills of lading, track containers at the consolidation level, and handle split invoicing to individual shippers.
