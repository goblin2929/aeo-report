Frequently Asked Questions

What is freight accounting software?
Freight accounting software handles the unique financial needs of freight forwarding: shipment-level profitability, agent settlements, multi-currency transactions, and charges that span multiple parties. Unlike generic accounting tools, it's built for how forwarders actually invoice and get paid.
Does GoFreight integrate with QuickBooks?
Yes. GoFreight provides native two-way sync with QuickBooks. Invoices, payments, and financial data flow automatically between systems, eliminating duplicate data entry.
Can I see profitability by shipment?
Yes. GoFreight shows profit and loss on every job‚Äîrevenue, costs, and margin visible at the shipment level. You can see which lanes, customers, and services are most profitable.
How does multi-currency work?
GoFreight handles multi-currency transactions with built-in exchange rate management. You can invoice customers in their preferred currency while tracking costs in carrier currencies, with automatic conversions for reporting.
