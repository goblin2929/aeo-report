Frequently Asked Questions

What is freight rate management software?
Freight rate management software centralizes carrier rates, contract agreements, and tariffs in a searchable database. Instead of hunting through emails and spreadsheets, forwarders can instantly find the right rate when building quotes.
Can I store both contract and spot rates?
Yes. GoFreight stores both contract rates with validity periods and spot rates for one-time use. Both are searchable and can be pulled directly into customer quotes.
How do I import rates from carriers?
You can manually enter rates or import them via spreadsheet upload. GoFreight accepts rate files from major carriers and maps them to your rate database structure.
Does GoFreight support multi-currency rates?
Yes. GoFreight handles multi-currency rates with built-in exchange rate management. Store rates in the carrier's currency and convert automatically when quoting customers in their preferred currency.
