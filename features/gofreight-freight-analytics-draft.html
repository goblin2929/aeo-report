Frequently Asked Questions

What is freight analytics software?
Freight analytics software provides visibility into forwarding operations performance‚Äîprofitability by shipment, customer, and lane, carrier performance metrics, and cost trends. It transforms operational data into actionable insights for better decision-making.
Can I see profitability by shipment?
Yes. GoFreight calculates profit and loss on every shipment automatically. You can see revenue, costs, and margin at the job level, and aggregate by customer, lane, or time period.
What reports does GoFreight provide?
GoFreight includes built-in reports for P&L by shipment, customer profitability, lane analysis, AR/AP aging, shipment volumes, and carrier performance. You can also build custom reports to match your specific needs.
Can I export data to Excel or other tools?
Yes. GoFreight supports data export to Excel and CSV formats. You can export any report or create custom data extracts for analysis in external tools or BI platforms.
