Frequently Asked Questions

What is air freight management software?
Air freight management software provides freight forwarders with specialized tools for air cargo operations‚Äîhouse and master air waybill generation, flight tracking, air consolidation workflows, and compliance features like AES filing for exports.
Can I generate HAWB and MAWB documents?
Yes. GoFreight generates both House Air Waybills (HAWB) and Master Air Waybills (MAWB) directly from shipment data. Documents are formatted correctly and can be printed or sent electronically.
How does flight tracking work?
GoFreight tracks air shipments through their journey‚Äîdeparture, arrival, and key milestones. Status updates flow into the system, and you can set alerts to notify your team and customers of flight status changes.
Does GoFreight support air consolidations?
Yes. GoFreight supports air consolidation workflows, allowing you to group multiple shippers under a single MAWB. House waybills, master waybills, and split invoicing are all handled in the system.
