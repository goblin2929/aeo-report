2. SEO definition block — "What is ocean freight management software?"

Place: directly under hero, before Triple Trap section
Why it matters: The exact format Google AI Overviews and ChatGPT cite for category-defining queries. A short, scannable definition placed near the top of the page wins the featured snippet for "ocean freight management software" and the long-tail variants ("ocean freight software vs TMS", "what does ocean freight management software do"). Without it, competitor definition blocks take that real estate instead.
