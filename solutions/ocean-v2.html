Frequently Asked Questions

What is ocean freight management software?
Ocean freight management software gives freight forwarders specialized tools for managing sea cargo operations end to end. This includes FCL and LCL shipment management, container tracking across 125+ carriers, consolidation workflows, ocean documentation like bills of lading and ISF filings, and agent settlements. Unlike generic logistics tools, it connects quoting, operations, compliance, and billing in one platform built for how ocean forwarders actually work.
Does GoFreight handle both FCL and LCL shipments?
Yes. GoFreight manages Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) shipments with full consolidation workflow support. You can group multiple shippers, manage house and master bills, and handle split invoicing all in one system.
How does GoFreight reduce demurrage and detention costs?
GoFreight sends proactive alerts when containers approach free time expiration, giving your team time to arrange pickup before fees hit. Automated milestone tracking across 125+ carriers means no more missed events. One GoFreight customer reduced D&D costs by over 53% after implementing automated container alerts.
Can GoFreight handle both ocean import and export?
Yes. GoFreight provides dedicated workflows for both directions. Ocean import includes container tracking, ISF filing, and arrival notices. Ocean export includes rate management, AES filing, and carrier booking. Both sides share a single database, so quoting, operations, compliance, and billing are fully connected.
How does ocean freight quoting work in GoFreight?
GoFreight stores contract and spot rates in a centralized database. When a rate request comes in, your team searches by lane, carrier, or service level, applies markups, and generates a professional quote all in minutes. When the customer accepts, one click converts the quote to a live shipment with all data carried over. No re entry required.
Does GoFreight integrate with customs filing for ocean shipments?
Yes. GoFreight connects directly to CBP's ACE system for ISF (10+2) filings on imports and AES filings on exports. Shipment data auto populates the filing forms. You review, adjust if needed, and submit without switching systems.
