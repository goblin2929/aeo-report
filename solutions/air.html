Frequently Asked Questions

What is air freight management software?
Air freight management software provides freight forwarders with specialized tools for air cargo operations: house and master air waybill generation, flight tracking, air consolidation workflows, and compliance features like AES filing for exports. Unlike generic logistics tools, it connects quoting, operations, documentation, and billing in one platform built for how air forwarders actually work.
Can I generate HAWB and MAWB documents?
Yes. GoFreight generates both House Air Waybills (HAWB) and Master Air Waybills (MAWB) directly from shipment data. Documents are formatted correctly and can be printed or sent electronically.
How does flight tracking work in GoFreight?
GoFreight tracks air shipments through their journey: departure, arrival, and key milestones. Status updates flow into the system, and you can set alerts to notify your team and customers of flight status changes.
Does GoFreight support air consolidations?
Yes. GoFreight supports air consolidation workflows, allowing you to group multiple shippers under a single MAWB. House waybills, master waybills, and split invoicing are all handled in the system.
How does GoFreight help air freight quoting?
GoFreight centralizes contract and spot rates in one searchable database. Your team can build professional quotes in seconds, respond faster than competitors, and convert won quotes into live shipments with one click. Forwarders using GoFreight reply up to 47% faster on air freight inquiries.
Does GoFreight integrate with AES filing for air exports?
Yes. GoFreight includes direct AES (Automated Export System) filing for air exports. Export shipment data flows directly into the filing, and you receive confirmation through the system. No need to switch between platforms.
