Frequently Asked Questions

What is air freight management software?
Air freight management software is a platform that helps freight forwarders manage air cargo shipments from booking to delivery. It includes AWB automation, compliance filing (AES), rate management, and customer communication tools.
Can GoFreight generate MAWB and HAWB automatically?
Yes. GoFreight auto-generates Master Air Waybills (MAWB) and House Air Waybills (HAWB) from shipment data using pre-set templates. AWB number sequences are maintained automatically.
Does GoFreight support e-AWB transmission?
Yes. GoFreight can send electronic AWB data directly to airlines via EDI integration, reducing manual entry at cargo acceptance and speeding up the shipping process.
Can I file AES directly from GoFreight?
Yes. GoFreight includes direct AES (Automated Export System) filing for U.S. air exports. Data flows from your shipment record into the filing, and you can track submission status within the platform.
