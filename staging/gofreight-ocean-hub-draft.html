Frequently Asked Questions

What is ocean freight management software?
Ocean freight management software is a platform that helps freight forwarders manage ocean shipments from booking to delivery. It includes container tracking, documentation, customs compliance (ISF/AES), rate management, and customer communication tools.
Does GoFreight handle both FCL and LCL shipments?
Yes. GoFreight manages both Full Container Load (FCL) and Less than Container Load (LCL) shipments, including consolidation workflows, container-level tracking, and split billing.
Can I file ISF directly from GoFreight?
Yes. GoFreight includes direct ISF (Importer Security Filing / 10+2) filing for U.S. ocean imports. Data flows from your shipment record into the filing, reducing manual entry and errors.
How does container tracking work?
GoFreight tracks containers through their entire lifecycleâ€”vessel departure, transshipment, arrival, customs clearance, and delivery. Automated alerts notify your team and customers of key milestones and potential delays.
